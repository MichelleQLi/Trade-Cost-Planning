# Hydraulic - Water Supply
## **Defination**
  - ### In New Zealand, a water supply system is typically defined as the facilities that provide drinking water and industrial water, including water source collection, water treatment, distribution, storage, monitoring, and maintenance. The primary objective of these systems is to deliver safe, clean, and dependable drinking water to meet the needs of communities and industries.
